Context

[Draft — full copy coming] Built during a design-a-thon to face the elephant in the room — how AI is reshaping creative work — by celebrating human, cultural craft. Nexus is a Pinterest-style hub for discovering art across cultures and traditions.

Where we began

As AI floods the feed, where does cultural craft go?

We wanted a space that points artists back to roots — the traditions, techniques, and cultures behind the work — instead of an endless stream of synthetic sameness.

concept · framing the problem

What we found

Discovery feels richer when it’s rooted, not random.

Inspiration meant more when it carried context: where a style came from, who made it, what it represents. People wanted to explore by culture and tradition, not just by aesthetic.

“Add a judge or user quote here.”

Solution

A visual hub, organized by culture and craft.

Nexus is a richly visual, browsable board of inspiration grouped by culture, region, and tradition — the kind of place an artist can fall into and come out knowing something. The visual design is what won the room.
