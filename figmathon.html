Context

[Draft — full copy coming] Highway hypnosis is the dreamlike state drivers drift into on long, monotonous roads. Time we can’t get back — and sometimes safety we can’t either. We built a phone app paired with a device to detect that state and gently pull drivers back.

Where we began

The most dangerous moments feel like nothing at all.

Highway hypnosis is hard to notice from the inside — that’s what makes it dangerous. We started by understanding the signals that someone has slipped into autopilot.

research · signals of the dreamlike state

What we found

An alert has to wake you — without startling you.

Too harsh and you swerve; too soft and it’s ignored. The design challenge was an intervention that re-engages attention calmly and at exactly the right moment.

“Add a test or judge quote here.”

Solution

Sense the drift, restore attention, gently.

The connected device watches for the dreamlike state and the app responds with a calibrated, calm nudge that brings focus back to the road — buying back the time and attention the state quietly steals.
